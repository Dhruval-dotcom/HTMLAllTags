colgroup tag

The <colgroup> tag specifies a group of one or more columns in a table for formatting. 
    <table>
        <colgroup>
            <col span="2" style="background-color:red">
            <col style="background-color:yellow">
        </colgroup>
        <tr>
            <th>ISBN</th>
            <th>Title</th>
            <th>Price</th>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td>3476896</td>
            <td>My first HTML</td>
            <td>$53</td>
        </tr>
    </table>
